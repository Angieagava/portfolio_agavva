ZSP / WCS

Coding School Webpage

Landing webpage designed to grab the visitor’s attention, present them with a specific service, and then encourage them to take a desired action: pressing a call to action button navigating towards a purchess. It collects leads under two forms.

Overview

Description : Landing pages are created for a specific business purpose: get more users, increase sales of the product. Landing pages are the best way to promote your product or service.

I listed the most important elements a landing page must-have:

  • Clear Call-to-Action (CTA).
  • Have only one conversion goal like increasing course sales.
  • Provide testimonials to support the credibility of the business.
  • Include benefits the courses are providing.

Project details Business goal — increase course sales. Conversion goal — the visitors click on the CTA to sign up for the course. Understand the users — the age group between 18–30 tend to have more stress, anxieties, and depression.
