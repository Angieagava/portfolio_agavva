Overview

Landing pages are essential for businesses looking to promote their product or service and drive more conversions. These pages serve a specific purpose - to increase user engagement and product sales.

To achieve these goals, landing pages must include key elements that capture the attention of visitors and persuade them to take action.

Here are the most crucial elements for an effective landing page:

  1. A clear and prominent call-to-action (CTA). This is the button or link that prompts visitors to take the desired action, such as signing up for a course.
  2. A focused conversion goal. It's crucial to have a single, clear goal for the landing page, such as increasing course sales.
  3. Highlighting the benefits of the course. The landing page should clearly communicate the value that the course provides to potential students.

In this particular project, the business goal is to increase course sales, and the conversion goal is to have visitors click on the CTA to sign up for the course. By including these crucial elements, the landing page can effectively promote the course and drive more conversions.
